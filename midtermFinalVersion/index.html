Tetris Recreation

This game is a recreation of Tetris. The inspiration was really simple: it is a game full of childhood memories.
Also, this game can also teach players to harmonize different interests and needs into a whole, and then eliminate them.

Here are some illustrations:

1. Timing -- The player needs to anticipate when the action will be needed.

2. Reflexes -- The player needs to make the right input when things and situations arise in a way that they can't predict

3. Resource Management -- Players need to integrate and manage their limited resources

4. Analytical skills -- The player needs to gather information, make internal logical connections, and find solutions to problems

Enjoy!
